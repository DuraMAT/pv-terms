PV Modeling Glossary¶

Terms are grouped by category.

Abbreviations¶

These abbreviations are used without expansion in the rest of the variables.

  • ac: alternating current
  • aod: aerosol optical depth
  • aoi: angle of incidence
  • coeff: coefficient
  • dc: direct current
  • dhi: direct horizontal irradiance
  • dni: direct normal irradiance
  • ghi: global horizontal irradiance
  • imp: current at maximum power
  • inv: inverter
  • isc: current at short circuit
  • max: maximum
  • min: minimum
  • mp: maximum power
  • mppt: maximum power point tracking
  • noct: nominal operating cell temperature
  • num: number
  • oc: open circuit
  • pmp: power at maximum power
  • poa: plane of array (irradiance)
  • ref: reference
  • sc: short circuit
  • stc: standard test conditions
  • vmp: voltage at maximum power
  • voc: voltage at open circuit

Optional Suffixes¶

These optional suffixes can be appended to a variable in order to further clarify which quantity the variable refers to. In the following * refers to another variable.

  • *_sim: Parameter predicted from a model, e.g. voc_sim
  • *_meas: Parameter measured from field data, e.g. voc_meas
  • *_rated: Parameter on the datasheet or as-built.
  • *_module: Quantity relating to a single PV module
  • *_string: Quantity relating to a string of PV modules.
  • *_array: Quantity relating to a number of PV strings.

Location¶

These variables are associated with the location of the PV system.

  • tz: timezone
  • latitude: latitude, in degrees
  • longitude: longitude, in degrees
  • elevation: elevation of system, in meters
  • pressure: pressure, in pascal
  • albedo: Ground reflectance, unitless. Typically 0.1-0.4 for surfaces on Earth (land), may increase over snow, ice, etc. May also be known as the reflection coefficient. Must be >=0 and <=1.

Atmosphere¶

Atmosphere variables are related to weather conditions.

  • airmass: airmass
  • airmass_relative: relative airmass
  • airmass_absolute: absolute airmass
  • dew_point: dewpoint temperature, in celsius.
  • relative_humidity: Relative humidity. The ratio of the partial pressure of water vapor to the equilibrium vapor pressure of water at a given temperature, unitless.
  • specific_humidity:
  • absolute_humidity:
  • total_precipitable_water:
  • wind_speed: Wind speed measured at standard 10 meter height, in m/s
  • wind_direction:

Solar Position¶

These variables are related to the position of sun and the incidence angles with the PV array.

  • aoi: angle of incidence between 90deg90deg and 90deg90deg, in degrees
  • aoi_projection: cos(aoi)
  • apparent_solar_elevation: refraction-corrected solar elevation angle in degrees
  • apparent_solar_zenith: refraction-corrected solar zenith angle, in degrees
  • solar_azimuth: azimuth angle of the sun in degrees East of North
  • solar_elevation: elevation angle of the sun in degrees
  • solar_zenith: zenith angle of the sun in degrees
  • surface_azimuth: Surface azimuth angles in decimal degrees. The azimuth convention is defined as degrees east of north (e.g. North=0, South=180, East=90, West=270)
  • surface_tilt: Surface tilt angles in decimal degrees. The tilt angle is defined as degrees from horizontal (e.g. surface facing up = 0, surface facing horizon = 90)

DC Output¶

These variables are related to the DC side of a PV power plant.

  • vmp: voltage at the maximum power point in Volts [Deprecated/alternates: v_mp]
  • imp: current at the maximum power point in Volts [Deprecated/alternates: i_mp]
  • pmp: power at the maximum power point in Volts [Deprecated/alternates: p_mp]
  • voc: open circuit module voltage [Deprecated/alternates: v_oc, voltage_oc]
  • isc: short circuit module current [Deprecated/alternates: i_sc]
  • current_x: Sandia Array Performance Model IV curve parameter [Deprecated/alternates: i_x]
  • current_xx: Sandia Array Performance Model IV curve parameter [Deprecated/alternates: i_xx]
  • power_dc: dc power

AC Output¶

These variables are related to the AC side of a PV power plant.

  • power_ac: ac power, in watts.

Inverter¶

These parameters are related to the operation of inverters.

  • max_inv_ac_current: Maximum inverter AC output current, in amps.
  • max_inv_ac_power: Maximum inverter AC output power, in watts.
  • max_inv_ac_voltage: Maximum inverter AC output voltage, in volts.
  • max_inv_dc_current: Maximum inverter DC input current, in amps.
  • max_inv_dc_voltage: Maximum inverter dc input voltage, in volts.
  • max_total_harmonic_distortion: Maximum total harmonic distortion of inverter AC output, dimensionless.
  • min_inv_ac_voltage: Minimum inverter AC ouptut voltage, in volts
  • min_inv_start_voltage: Minimum inverter input DC voltage to start operation, also called the inverter start voltage, in volts.
  • min_mppt_voltage: Minimum inverter input DC voltage to engage maximum power point tracking, in volts.
  • nominal_inv_ac_voltage: Nominal inverter AC output voltage, in volts.
  • num_mppt_circuits: Number of MPPT circuits, dimensionless
  • min_inv_operating_temperature: Minimum inverter operating temperature, in celsius.
  • max_inv_operating_temperature: Maximum inverter operating temperature, in celsius.

Irradiance¶

These variables denote irradiance in units of W/m^2.

  • dhi: diffuse horizontal irradiance
  • dni: direct normal irradiance
  • dni_extra: direct normal irradiance at top of atmosphere (extraterrestrial)
  • effective_irradiance: irradiance reaching the module’s cells, i.e., in the plane of array, reduced by soiling and reflections, adjusted for spectrum
  • ghi: global horizontal irradiance
  • poa_diffuse: total diffuse irradiation in plane. sum of ground and sky diffuse.
  • poa_direct: direct/beam irradiation in plane
  • poa_global: global irradiation in plane. sum of diffuse and beam projection.
  • poa_ground_diffuse: in plane ground reflected irradiation
  • poa_sky_diffuse: diffuse irradiation in plane from scattered light in the atmosphere (without ground reflected irradiation)
  • transposition_factor: the gain ratio of the radiation on inclined plane to global horizontal irradiation: poa_global/ghi

PV System¶

These variables are related to the operating current, voltage and power of PV cells, modules or arrays.

  • array_tilt: tilt angle of the surface, in degrees [Deprecated/alternates: surface_tilt]
  • array_azimuth: azimuth angle of the surface, in degrees [Deprecated/alternates: surface_azimuth]
  • temperature_cell: temperature of the cell, in C
  • temperature_module: temperature of the module, in C
  • temperature_air: ambient dry bulb temperature, in C
  • efficiency_inverter: inverter efficiency
  • efficiency_inverter_ref: reference inverter efficiency
  • efficiency_inverter_nominal: nominal inverter efficiency
  • spectral_mismatch: The ratio of power produced by a PV cell with a particular spectral distribution of light divided by the power produced by a reference spectrum, unitless.
  • modules_per_string: Number of modules in series in each string
  • parallel_strings: Number of parallel strings in an array of modules
  • tracker_type: Tracker type, can be ‘fixed’, ‘single-axis’ or ‘two-axis’
  • ground_coverage_ratio: A value denoting the ground coverage ratio of a tracker system which utilizes backtracking; i.e. the ratio between the PV array surface area to total ground area. A tracker system with modules 2 meters wide, centered on the tracking axis, with 6 meters between the tracking axes has a gcr of 2/6=0.333. If gcr is not provided, a gcr of 2/7 is default. gcr must be <=1.

Module Parameters¶

Module parameters are primarily used to describe the electrical characteristic of a PV module.

  • pmp_ref: Power at maximum power and reference conditions for a PV cell, module or array, in W.
  • alpha_isc: The temperature coefficient of short-circuit current for a PV module, in A/C [Deprecated/alternates: alpha_sc]
  • beta_voc: Temperature coefficient of open-circuit voltage in V/C
  • gamma_pmp: Temperature coefficient of power at maximum power point, in W/C [Deprecated/alternates: gamma_pdc]
  • gamma_vmp: Temperature coefficient of voltage at maximum power point, in V/C
  • gamma_imp: Temperature coefficient of voltage at maximum power point, in A/C
  • photocurrent: photocurrent
  • photocurrent_ref: The light-generated current (or photocurrent) at reference conditions, in amperes [Deprecated/alternates: I_L_ref]
  • saturation_current: diode saturation current, in amperes
  • saturation_current_ref: The dark or diode reverse saturation current at reference conditions, in amperes [Deprecated/alternates: I_o_ref]
  • resistance_series: series resistance, in Ohms
  • resistance_series_ref: Series resistance at reference conditions, in Ohms [Deprecated/alternates: R_s]
  • resistance_shunt: shunt resistance, in Ohms
  • resistance_shunt_ref: shunt resistance at reference conditions, in Ohms.
  • voltage_thermal: Thermal voltage per cell in Volts, equal to k_B*T/q where k_B is the boltzman constant (in J/K), T is the temperature (in Kelvin) and q is the electron charge (in Coulombs).
  • nNsVth_ref: The product of the usual diode ideality factor (n, unitless), number of cells in series (Ns), and cell thermal voltage at reference conditions, in units of V.
  • nNsVth: The product of the usual diode ideality factor (n, unitless), number of cells in series (Ns), and cell thermal voltage, in units of V.
  • num_cells_in_series: Number of cells in series in a PV module, unitless
  • diode_factor: diode ideality factor, unitless
  • adjust_cec: CEC module parameter [Deprecated/alternates: adjust]
  • area_module: module area
  • noct: Nominal operating cell temperature.
  • band_gap_ref: Energy bandgap at reference temperature in units of eV. 1.121 eV for crystalline silicon. EgRef must be >0. For parameters from the SAM CEC module database, EgRef=1.121 is implicit for all cell types in the parameter estimation algorithm used by NREL.
  • irradiance_ref: Reference irradiance in W/m^2. Typical value is 1000 W/m^2.
  • temperature_ref: Reference temperature in C. Typical value is 25 C
  • band_gap_temperature_coeff: The temperature dependence of the energy bandgap at reference conditions in units of 1/K. [Deprecated/alternates: dEgdT]
